Privacy Policy

ZERO personal data collected

Last updated: January 30, 2025

Our Privacy Promise

  • We do NOT collect personal data
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT create user profiles
  • We do NOT sell any data
  • We do NOT require registration or account
  • We do NOT save your history

What information is used?

Only the information YOU provide when making a report:

  • Report location (where you saw ICE activity)
  • Report time
  • Optional note (if you choose to add one)

This information is anonymous and automatically deleted after 5 hours.

Your Location

Your location is NEVER sent to our servers. It's used only on your device to:

  • Show you on the map
  • Filter nearby reports
  • Calculate distances

All location processing happens locally on your phone.

Storage

  • Reports: Saved in Firebase for 5 hours, then deleted
  • Preferences: Saved only on your device
  • No cookies, trackers, or analytics

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@red-dot.app
